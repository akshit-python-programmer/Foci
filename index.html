Foci — focus without losing time

Everything in one window

Calm by design

No streaks to break, no red numbers shouting at you. Just gentle structure that makes the day feel possible.

🗓️

Visual day timeline

Drag on the grid to create events, drag to move or resize them. The background gently shifts from dawn to golden hour to night as the real clock moves.

✅

Nested to-do list

Flat tasks or collapsible folders, with simple today / tomorrow / someday tags that quietly roll forward on their own. Drag to reorder.

⏱️

Focus timer

One click on an active task opens a big, distraction-free countdown with a live progress ring so you always know where you are.

🎵

Built-in lofi player

A calm soundtrack baked right in, with proper play / pause / skip — and it even responds to your keyboard's media keys.

🎉

Small, kind rewards

Earn coins and a little confetti when you finish something. Encouragement instead of guilt, so momentum builds on its own.

📊

Gentle stats

Soft donut charts show what you actually got done today, and a weekly view lets you glance across the whole week at once.

🎨

Themes & fonts

Eight handmade themes from Serene Morning to Cyberpunk Night, several with breathing animated gradients. Pick the mood that fits your day.

🔒

Local & private

Everything lives in plain files on your computer. No cloud, no tracking, nothing uploaded. Optional Google Calendar sync if you want it.
